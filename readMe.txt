Shape Arena

A fighting game where one tries to bring their opponents health to 0.

Controls:

Player 1:
	up: w
	down: s
	right: d
	left: a 
	skill 1: c
	skill 2: v
	skill 3: b

Player 2:
	up: i
	down: k
	right: l
	left: j                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       
	skill 1: .
	skill 2: /
	skill 3: shi
